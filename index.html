How to Use

Follow these simple steps for best results

01

Wet Hair

Thoroughly wet your hair with lukewarm water before application

02

Apply & Massage

Take small amount, apply to wet hair & scalp, massage gently for 1 minute

03

Leave for 2-3 Minutes

Allow the herbal formula to work its magic for 2-3 minutes

04

Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair completely with clean water until all shampoo is removed

Pro Tip: For best results, use 2-3 times a week. Avoid using hot water as it can strip natural oils from your hair.
